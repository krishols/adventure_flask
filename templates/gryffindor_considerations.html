Of the Gryffindor house, you are... How this may affect your disclosure process:
Brave Use your confidence and bravery to your advantage. Have faith in yourself and whatever decision you make.
Independent Even though you may be self-sufficient, there is no reason to feel shame for seeking support from others. It is okay to share parts of your life with others if you are comfortable with it.
Defensive Regardless of what you believe is right, while you disclose you will have to acknowledge dissenting opinions. Try to be open-minded and consider other people's perspectives.
Reckless Don't act without thinking first, regardless of what your gut may tell you. Think it through and don't make rash decisions.
Arrogant Don't approach disclosing with a pompous attitude. Try to be humble and honest.
